// WHY LOCAL

The cloud is someone else's computer.
Yours doesn't have to be.

Modern AI can read, write, sort, and summarize most of the paperwork that slows a business down. But if your client contracts, compliance obligations, or trade secrets say data can't leave your control, the big cloud AI providers are off the table. Vaulterom exists for exactly that gap: serious AI capability, running on a machine in your office.

Easier to keep your compliance

No product makes you compliant out of the box — and we won't pretend ours does. What a local machine removes is the third party: your data never leaves systems you control, so the rules and contracts you already operate under are simpler to uphold.

Your IP stays yours

Designs, formulas, source code, pricing models. Cloud AI terms change; your machine doesn't. Prompts, documents, and outputs never touch a third-party server — and are never anyone's training data.

One machine, predictable cost

No per-token bills, no surprise rate hikes, no usage caps in the middle of a busy week. You buy the hardware once and run it as hard as you want.
